What Is Bad Mutha Forker?

Bad Mutha Forker is a free AI-powered recipe generator built for people who are obsessed with street food — the kind that comes from taco trucks in East LA, ramen stalls in Shinjuku, jerk chicken shacks in Kingston, and night market carts in Bangkok. The kind of food that doesn't have a Michelin star but has a line around the block.

We built it because home cooks deserve access to authentic global flavors without a culinary school degree or an international flight. Type in what you're craving, and Bad Mutha Forker serves up a full recipe — ingredients, technique, pro tips — tailored to real street food tradition.

🌍 50+ cuisines. Instant output. Zero cost. Whether you want to replicate the bulgogi sliders you had at that food truck festival or finally nail your grandmother's jollof rice, this is your AI sous-chef for global street food.
